Our Commitment to Fairness

Provably Random

Each flip utilizes the browser's `crypto.getRandomValues()` API, a cryptographically secure pseudo-random number generator (CSPRNG). This provides a much higher degree of randomness than standard `Math.random()`, ensuring results are unpredictable and unbiased.

Transparent Process

Our code is client-side and fully auditable. You can view the page source to see exactly how the random number is generated and translated into a "Heads" or "Tails" outcome. There are no server-side manipulations or hidden variables.
